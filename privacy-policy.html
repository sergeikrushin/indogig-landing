1. INTRODUCTION

Welcome to Indogig. This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how PT INDOGIG HRTECH INDONESIA ("Indogig," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects the personal data of users who access and use the Indogig platform, including our website, mobile applications, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

PT INDOGIG HRTECH INDONESIA is a company focused on HR technology, registered and based at Jalan Sunset Road Tengah Building No. 9, Seminyak, Kuta, Badung, Bali, Indonesia. Indogig is officially registered in Indonesia under registration number AHU-0090431.AH.01.01.Tahun 2023 dated November 27, 2023, NIB 2911230067256, and holds Sertifikat Standar KBLI 78104 (Aktivitas Penyediaan Tenaga Kerja Lainnya) as well as TD PSE Domestik registration with the Ministry of Communication and Digital Affairs (Komdigi).

Indogig operates a workforce platform connecting businesses ("Companies") with part-time, temporary, and gig-based workers ("Workers") for shift-based work opportunities in Indonesia. Indogig acts as a technology platform that facilitates labor supply connections between Companies and Workers.

In the context of the Personal Data Protection Law No. 27 of 2022 ("UU PDP"), users of the Services are Data Subjects (Subjek Data Pribadi), and PT INDOGIG HRTECH INDONESIA acts as the Data Controller (Pengendali Data Pribadi). When Companies access Worker data through the platform, Companies act as independent Data Controllers for the data they process in connection with their own hiring and employment decisions.

By accessing or using our Services, you agree to the collection, use, retention, and disclosure of your information as described in this Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the Services.

6. SELFIE AND PHOTO DATA (SPECIFIC PERSONAL DATA)

Selfie photos and video presentations are classified as Specific Personal Data (Data Pribadi yang Bersifat Spesifik) under UU PDP Art. 4(2), as they contain biometric-capable facial data. We apply heightened protections to this data:

6.1 Check-in and Check-out Selfies

As part of our shift verification system, Workers are required to take selfie photos during check-in (before the shift begins) and check-out (after the shift ends). Separate explicit consent is collected at the point of first selfie capture. These photos:

  • Are used solely for attendance verification and shift time tracking
  • Are viewable by the Company that posted the shift and by Indogig administrators
  • Are stored securely with access controls limiting who can view them
  • Are retained for 3 years from the shift date (see Section 7)
  • Are not used for facial recognition technology, biometric profiling, surveillance, or any purpose other than attendance verification
  • May be used as evidence in payment or attendance disputes between Companies and Workers

6.2 Profile Photos and Video Presentations

  • Profile photos uploaded during onboarding are displayed on your Worker profile and are visible to Companies
  • Video self-presentations (up to 1 minute) are reviewed by Indogig administrators for quality and appropriateness, and may be viewable by Companies as part of the Worker's profile
  • Separate explicit consent is collected when you first upload a video presentation
  • You may update, replace, or delete your profile photo and video at any time through the app

  • Right to Information (Art. 5-7): Be informed about the identity of the Data Controller, the legal basis for processing, the purpose of processing, and the retention period
  • Right to Access (Art. 8): Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Correction (Art. 8): Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Deletion (Art. 8): Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements
  • Right to Restrict Processing (Art. 10): Request that we limit the processing of your data in certain circumstances
  • Right to Data Portability (Art. 12): Request a copy of your data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format

We do not discriminate against users who exercise their privacy rights.

6. DATA SELFIE DAN FOTO (DATA PRIBADI YANG BERSIFAT SPESIFIK)

Foto selfie dan video presentasi diklasifikasikan sebagai Data Pribadi yang Bersifat Spesifik berdasarkan UU PDP Pasal 4 ayat (2), karena mengandung data wajah yang bersifat biometrik. Kami menerapkan perlindungan yang ditingkatkan terhadap data ini:

6.1 Selfie Check-in dan Check-out

Sebagai bagian dari sistem verifikasi shift, Pekerja diwajibkan mengambil foto selfie saat check-in dan check-out. Persetujuan eksplisit terpisah dikumpulkan pada saat pengambilan selfie pertama. Foto-foto ini:

  • Digunakan semata-mata untuk verifikasi kehadiran dan pelacakan waktu shift
  • Dapat dilihat oleh Perusahaan yang memasang shift dan oleh administrator Indogig
  • Disimpan dengan aman dengan kontrol akses yang membatasi siapa yang dapat melihatnya
  • Disimpan selama 3 tahun dari tanggal shift (lihat Bagian 7)
  • Tidak digunakan untuk teknologi pengenalan wajah, profiling biometrik, pengawasan, atau tujuan lain selain verifikasi kehadiran
  • Dapat digunakan sebagai bukti dalam sengketa pembayaran atau kehadiran antara Perusahaan dan Pekerja

6.2 Foto Profil dan Video Presentasi

  • Foto profil yang diunggah selama onboarding ditampilkan di profil Pekerja dan dapat dilihat oleh Perusahaan
  • Video presentasi diri (maksimal 1 menit) ditinjau oleh administrator Indogig untuk kualitas dan kelayakan, dan dapat dilihat oleh Perusahaan sebagai bagian dari profil Pekerja
  • Persetujuan eksplisit terpisah dikumpulkan saat Anda pertama kali mengunggah video presentasi
  • Anda dapat memperbarui, mengganti, atau menghapus foto profil dan video kapan saja melalui aplikasi

  • Hak Pembatasan Pemrosesan (Pasal 10): Meminta pembatasan pemrosesan data Anda dalam keadaan tertentu
