1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how PT INDOGIG HRTECH INDONESIA ("Indogig," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects the personal data of users who access and use the Indogig platform, including our website, mobile applications, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

PT INDOGIG HRTECH INDONESIA is registered at Jalan Sunset Road Tengah Building No. 9, Seminyak, Kuta, Badung, Bali, Indonesia (AHU-0090431.AH.01.01.Tahun 2023, NIB 2911230067256, Sertifikat Standar KBLI 78104, TD PSE Domestik).

Indogig operates a workforce platform connecting businesses ("Companies") with part-time, temporary, and gig-based workers ("Workers") for shift-based work opportunities in Indonesia. Indogig acts as a technology platform that facilitates labor supply connections between Companies and Workers.

Under UU PDP No. 27/2022, users are Data Subjects (Subjek Data Pribadi), and PT INDOGIG HRTECH INDONESIA acts as the Data Controller (Pengendali Data Pribadi).

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Account creation, authentication (WhatsApp OTP), and profile management
  • Job matching — connecting Workers with relevant shifts; Profile Strength affects visibility
  • Shift management — applications, hiring, check-in/out, completion
  • Payment processing — Company balance top-ups, Worker payment disbursements
  • Document verification — admin review of CVs and videos
  • Recommendation system and rating/reputation management
  • Communication — notifications, updates, support
  • Platform improvement — analytics, research, fraud prevention
  • Legal compliance — Indonesian labor, tax, and data protection laws

6. Selfie and Photo Data

Selfie photos and video presentations are classified as Specific Personal Data (Data Pribadi yang Bersifat Spesifik) under UU PDP Art. 4(2). Separate explicit consent is collected at the point of capture. Photos are:

  • Used solely for attendance verification
  • Viewable by the hiring Company and Indogig admins
  • Retained for 3 years from shift date
  • Not used for facial recognition or biometric profiling
